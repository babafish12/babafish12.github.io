A PARLEY WITH ANGELS

THE LATEST EP FROM FISH

The latest EP from Fish, ‘A Parley With Angels’ features the tracks – ‘Man With A Stick’, ‘Waverley Steps’ and ‘Little Man What Now?’, all of which will appear on Fish’s final studio album, Weltschmerz. Also included are four tracks recorded live at London’s Islington Assembly Hall in 2017: ‘Emperor’s Song’, ‘State Of Mind’, ‘Circle Line’ and ‘Voyeur’.
